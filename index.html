01. About Me

I'm a PhD Student at École de technologie supérieure (ÉTS) in Montreal, Canada, researching AI for Software Engineering and Agentic AI. Previously, I served as Head of Computer Center Department and Lecturer at the University College of Applied Sciences (UCAS) in Gaza, Palestine.

My research explores how AI agents can transform software engineering workflows — from understanding why developers reject AI-generated pull requests, to evaluating Large Language Models for automated essay scoring, to engineering morphology-aware prompts for Arabic NLP.

I believe technology should empower learners and developers everywhere. Whether I'm investigating agentic code changes, teaching the next generation of engineers, or publishing research on intelligent systems — that mission drives everything I do.
